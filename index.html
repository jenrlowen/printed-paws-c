The African Wild Dog holds unique attributes like no other animal, but they're slowly going extinct as a result of human interference.

What are African Wild Dogs?

African Wild Dogs are incredible creatures with capabilities like no other. They are extremely pack-oriented animals that look out for one another without fail. Their communication methods are beyond that of any other animal, allowing them to have the highest rate of success in hunting. Their diet typically consists of gazelles and warthogs.

What's Happening to Them?

African Wild Dogs are unfortunately a critically endangered species. There are various unfortunate reasons for their declining population, ranging from human interference to habitat loss. There are currently less than 7,000 dogs left in the wild.
